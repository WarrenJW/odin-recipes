Homemade Kentucky Fried Chicken

Description

This is a recipe you can make at home that tastes just like real KFC! Our recipe has unlocked the Colonel's secrets, now you can enjoy it for yourself by making it at home!

Ingredients

  • 600g plain flour
  • 4 tbsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp white pepper
  • tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp ground ginger
  • 1 tbsp ground ginger
  • 1 tbsp mustard powder
  • 1 tbsp celery
  • 1tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tbsp of oregano
  • ½ tbsp thyme

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 80C.
  2. Add the spices to the flour and mix well.
  3. Make sure the chicken is at room temperature before seasoning it in the flour and spice mix.
  4. After coating the chicken in seasoning, brush it with the egg white and milk wash before popping it back in the seasoning.
  5. Fry the chicken for five to six minutes at 160C before putting it in the preheated oven to keep warm.
  6. Fry again for 90 seconds before serving.
